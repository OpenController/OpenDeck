By default, all potentiometers are disabled to avoid sending of random data if one or more analogue inputs are left disconnected.

Enable all potentiometers:
F0 00 53 43 01 01 06 00 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 01 F7

Disable all potentiometers:
F0 00 53 43 01 01 06 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 F7

Disable potentiometer 3:
F0 00 53 43 01 00 06 00 03 00 F7

Enable potentiometer 3:
F0 00 53 43 01 00 06 00 03 01 F7
