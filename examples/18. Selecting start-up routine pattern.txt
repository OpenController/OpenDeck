There are currently 4 start-up routine patterns. In order to select start-up pattern, number of LEDs must be specified first.

Setting total LED number to 4:
F0 00 53 43 01 00 01 00 03 04 F7

Pattern 1:
F0 00 53 43 01 00 01 00 05 01 F7

Pattern 2:
F0 00 53 43 01 00 01 00 05 02 F7

Pattern 3:
F0 00 53 43 01 00 01 00 05 03 F7

Pattern 4:
F0 00 53 43 01 00 01 00 05 04 F7

Excepted paramters are 0-4. 0 means start-up routine is disabled.
